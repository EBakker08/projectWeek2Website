    About Cubic Productions

    We, Cubic Productions are a group of students from ROC Nijmegen in the Netherlands that have been rallied together for a project at school.

    For this project, we are creating a game called "Colour Scape". You can find more info about our game on the games page.

    The game is currently still in development, but there will be a demo available on itch.io soon.

    Our people

    We are a five-person team working on a school project to develop a game called "Colour Scape."

    Our team consists of three developers and two designers, working closely together to combine technical gameplay with clear and engaging visual design.

    Our practices

    We work together to create a game that is both fun and easy to play. We use modern development tools and techniques to ensure our game is engaging and accessible to all players of all ages and skill levels.

    The main engine we use to create our games is Godot. For our design work we mainly use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
